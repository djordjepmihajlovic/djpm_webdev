This was a project I was part of in the summer of 2023. I worked on the development of code for a 3D smectic liquid crystal simulation by finding suitable lagrangian multipliers and implementing them into C++ code originally developed by J. Paget and T. Shendruck; see their original paper on <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-023-36506-z">Complex Tensor Theory of Simple Smectics.</a>
